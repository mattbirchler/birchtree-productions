Multi-Platform

Write once, publish everywhere.

Publish to Mastodon, Threads, Bluesky, X, and LinkedIn simultaneously. The platform picker shows your actual @username for each connected account and uses each platform's real brand colors.

Weave warns you if you've exceeded the most restrictive character limit across your selected platforms. No switching between apps, no copy-pasting.

Mastodon Threads Bluesky X LinkedIn

Thread Composer

Threads that just work.

Triple-newline to split a post into a new thread item. No buttons, no drag-and-drop, just write naturally. Each post gets its own character counter that's aware of which platform has the tightest limit.

A visual thread connector between posts makes the structure obvious at a glance, and the character counter's progress ring shifts from gray to accent to orange to red as you approach the limit.

Smart Scheduling

Schedule in plain English.

"Tomorrow at 3pm", "in 2 hours", "next tuesday at 9am" — type it in plain English and it parses on-device using Apple Foundation Models. No network call, no privacy concern.

There's also a graphical date picker as a fallback, plus "Add to Queue" to let Typefully auto-schedule your post in the next available slot.

Built with SwiftUI. Spring animations, haptic feedback, dynamic sheets, and system colors that adapt to light and dark mode.
