# Birchtree Productions

Portfolio website for Birchtree Productions, LLC — an indie app development company founded in 2025 by Matt Birchler.

## About

Birchtree Productions creates handcrafted apps and content for people who believe technology should serve us, not the other way around. No algorithms, no tracking, no bloat — just focused tools that respect your time and attention.

## Apps

- **Quick Reviews** — Review the things you love and share them to social media. *MacStories Best New App of 2025*
- **ChapterPod** — Create professional podcast chapters with titles, images, and links while you listen — all on your Apple devices.
- **Quick Subtitles** — Transcribe audio and video to text or SRT subtitles using fast, private, on-device AI.
- **Quick Notes** — Turn your voice into high-quality text using Apple's on-device language models.
- **Best-o-Masto** — Catch up without getting sucked in. Mastodon, efficiently.

## Creative

- **A Better Computer** — YouTube channel with video essays and reviews exploring how technology can enhance our lives and creative work.
- **Birchtree** — Long-form blog writing about Apple, productivity, and the intersection of technology and creativity.
